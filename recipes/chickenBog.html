Chicken Bog

Description:

This is my family's traditional take on a South Carolina low country classic. Chicken Bog is a dish with african roots consisting of rice, chicken, and sausage. My family recipe ommits the sausage and uses yellow saffron rice.
Typical with this region's dishes, its beauty is found within its simplicity.

Ingredients:

  • Bone-in chicken
  • 1 package saffron rice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps:

  1. In a deep pot, or dutch oven, bring enough water to submerge the chicken to a boil.
  2. Once water is at a boil, add the chicken.
  3. Let chicken boil for approx. 15 minutes.
  4. Remove chicken and let cool.
  5. Save enough boiled water for cooking your rice. discard the excess.
  6. Once the chicken is cooled, remove the meat and shred it with your fingers.
  7. Add the shredded chicken, and bones back into the retained water, and bring to a boil.
  8. Add the saffron rice into the pot and cook as directed on the packaging
  9. Add salt and peppers to taste, let cool down, and enjoy!
  10. Best enjoyed with a glass of sweet tea.
