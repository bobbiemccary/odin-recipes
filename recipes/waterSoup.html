Water soup

Description:

Sometimes life can be tough, in trying times, water soup is what you need.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Ice cubes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Add pepper.
  5. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
  6. Garnish with ice cubes.
  7. Put on a silly hat and enjoy with a fork.
